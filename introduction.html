General Introduction

Chinese Culture and Art Exploration Platform


The goal of this project is to create a comprehensive platform that integrates multiple functions to promote the communication and exchange of Chinese culture and art in all aspects. The platform addresses the limitations of existing platforms, which often have single or isolated functionalities, by providing a unified solution that meets diverse user needs.

Our platform aims to:

  • Promote cultural engagement through features such as a sharing module, where users can upload, tag, and interact with artistic works in various formats (e.g., images, videos, audio).
  • Facilitate participation in cultural activities with a ticketing system and calendar feature that allows users to browse event details, purchase tickets, and sync events to personal calendars.
  • Foster learning and deeper cultural and art understanding by incorporating an advanced Knowledge Q&A module powered by Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) techniques, enabling interactive and knowledge-based exploration.

By integrating these innovative features, the project seeks to protect and disseminate cultural heritage, enhance user interaction, and provide a unique and well-rounded cultural experience that fills a significant gap in the current market.
